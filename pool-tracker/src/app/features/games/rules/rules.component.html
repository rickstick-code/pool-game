Rick's Rule Set

Everybody can play the game how they want, but if you need a quick rule set, you can use this one. These are not the official rules – they’re simple bar rules meant to make the game more fun.

Objective

The goal is to sink all of your balls (halves or solids) and then the black ball (8).

  • If you sink more of one type (e.g., 3 solids, 1 stripe), that becomes your type.
  • If you sink an equal number (e.g., 1 solid, 1 stripe), you keep shooting until you sink another ball. That type becomes yours.
  • If you sink none, it’s your opponent’s turn, using the same rules.

The winner of the previous game (where either player participated) starts the next game.

Fouls

  • You fail to hit your own ball type first with the white ball.
  • You hit your opponent’s ball or the black ball first.
  • You sink your opponent’s ball, or the white ball, at any time.

Consequence: Turn ends, opponent places the white ball anywhere.

Exception: On the break, it must be placed on the break line.

Winning the Game

  • The black ball must be sunk last, into the opposite pocket of your previous ball.
  • If a foul occurs while sinking the black ball (see Fouls) → you lose.
  • If the black ball is sunk before all your other balls → you lose.

Additional Rules

  • No three-cushion/wall rule in this tournament.
  • If the white ball touches a wall/border, you may move it away by one cue-handle thickness.

Edge Cases

For anything not covered here, refer to the official WPA rules: wpapool.com/rules
